# Engine Reminder

Daily English uses the same building process.

```
WHO

↓

ACTION

↓

TARGET

↓

DETAILS
```

Whether you are talking about work, family, shopping or travel, the architecture of the idea remains the same.

The vocabulary changes.

The thinking process does not.
