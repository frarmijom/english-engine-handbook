## Fundamental Question

> **How does English classify the world?**

---

## Cognitive Goal

After studying this engine, the learner should naturally ask:

> **Is English seeing this as a UNIT or as a MASS?**

without consciously thinking about grammar rules.

---

## Knowledge Covered

- Countable nouns
- Uncountable nouns
- Units vs. Mass
- Singular vs. Plural (introduction)
- much vs. many (introduction)
- Counting uncountable nouns
- Native mental model
- Spanish interference

---

## Skills Acquired

After mastering this engine, the learner should be able to:

- Classify new nouns.
- Detect incorrect pluralization.
- Use *much* and *many* more naturally.
- Understand why some nouns cannot be pluralized.
- Explain the difference to another learner.
